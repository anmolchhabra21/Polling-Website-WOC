ABOUT

The purpose of StrawPoll.vote is to showcase the importance of the voting system used in an election. While many voting rights activists advocate for the adoption of ranked voting, there aren't many tools to easily see the differences between electoral systems.

The different outcomes between voting systems when studying them can feel very abstract. By providing a way to interact with the different voting systems, hopefully they will feel more intuitive.
